A community-led group to support our cloud physics community in the UK

This new project was created by PhD students at the University of Oxford in 2025. The goal is to foster knowledge, collaboration and community amongst the UK’s world-leading cloud physics research institutions. Although our focus is on early-career researchers, we hope this effort supports lively and engaged interactions amongst researchers at all career stages in atmospheric sciences across the UK.

The symposium

To kick-off achieving this goal, we are hosting a two day symposium in Oxford - and you’re invited!

It has been rumored that back in the day, before COVID-19, there was such a thing as an annual cloud physics meeting in the UK. But whether or not that was indeed the case, there is surely no such meeting around today… Until now!

We hope that this symposium acts as a revival to domestic science meetings in our field. These subject-specific local meetings are vital in maintaining the UK’s place as a world leader in cloud and atmospheric physics. Particularly for us early career researchers, they introduce us to new ideas, projects, collaborators and future employers.

So register your interest, and join us at the University of Oxford for a two-day research symposium on 19-20 March 2026.

If you can’t attend this year, register for virtual participation and ensure that your research is visible to all.
